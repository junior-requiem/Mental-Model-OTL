Expandable Learning Layers

Setup (configuration before daily use)

Define who gets which policy pack: HCM Groups, entry and processing profiles, layout sets, time attributes, categories, and rule sets.

Runtime (daily entry and approval)

Workers and managers capture time through time cards, calendar, web clock, or device imports. OTL then validates, calculates, and routes approvals.

Downstream (transfer and consumption)

After required approvals, time reaches ready-to-transfer status and is handed off to payroll, project costing, project execution, extracts, or external consumers.
