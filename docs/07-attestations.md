# Time Attestations

## What They Are

Time Attestation Sets let workers confirm additional compliance-related statements while reporting time.

## Purpose

They are used to support policy and regulatory acknowledgement, such as break compliance or related declarations.

## Mental Model

Attestations are the compliance questionnaire layer of OTL.

## How They Fit

- Linked to Worker Time Entry Profiles
- Can be triggered on Save or Submit
- Can be scoped using Time Categories

## Common Mistakes

- Setting display level too aggressively
- Prompting on every row instead of at a meaningful checkpoint
- Failing to align attestation logic to the right categories
